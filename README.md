## 🎯 What is Skapp?

Skapp is an **open-source people management and HR platform** designed for growing teams and startups. It provides essential HR tools including employee directory, leave management, and attendance tracking — all in a modern, easy-to-use interface.

**🏆 Award-Winning:** Gold Award Winner at APICTA 2025 (Asia Pacific ICT Alliance Awards)

## ✨ Features

### 📋 People Management (HR)
- Employee directory & profiles
- Organization hierarchy visualization
- Team structure management
- Employee onboarding workflows

### 🏖️ Leave Management
- Multiple leave types configuration
- Leave request & approval workflows
- Leave balance tracking
- Holiday calendar management
- Team leave calendar view

### ⏱️ Attendance & Time Tracking
- Clock in/out functionality
- Timesheet management
- Attendance reports & analytics
- Manual time entry adjustments
